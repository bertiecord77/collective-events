Our Story

COLLECTIVE. was born from a simple observation: the East Midlands has an incredible creative and tech community, but too often we work in silos. We wanted to change that.

Starting in Nottingham, we've grown to bring our unique brand of community events to Mansfield and Chesterfield. Each location has its own character, but the mission remains the same: create spaces where people can share ideas, learn from each other, and build meaningful professional relationships.

We're not just another networking event. We're building something different - a community where authenticity matters, where every voice is valued, and where the best conversations happen over a drink after the main event.

What We Stand For

Authenticity

Local Focus

We're proud to champion the East Midlands. Our events celebrate local talent and create opportunities within our region.

Growth Mindset

We're constantly learning and evolving. Each event teaches us something new about what our community needs.
